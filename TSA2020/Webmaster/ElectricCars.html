Our Solution

Electric cars can help save both peoples financial situations as well as the worlds environmental situations. By using electric cars rather than gas powered cars, there will be a significant decrease in air pollution, as well as letting people keep that extra change in their pockets instead of spending it on gas to refuel their car.

Electric cars are a big jump into helping provide a better future for not only those to come in the future, but for ourselves right now. Electric cars are one of the biggest environmental impact changes that humans have made to help prevent global warming from escelating to an uncontrollable level.
