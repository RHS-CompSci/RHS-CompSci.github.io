Our TSA Chapter

The TSA chapter at Rudder High School helps support students to engage in different areas to find what suits them the best, and find what they enjoy. Students spend hours a day working on projects that help them learn, and encourage them to learn new skills and methods to accomplish their goals.

Rudder High School TSA supports teamwork, communication, self-motivation, and management to help our students learn through real world situations and competitions that make our students want to engage in activities outside of school.

What Is TSA?

The Technology Student Association (TSA) is a national orgnization designed to create future leaders in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM) fields. The TSA motto "learning to lead a technical world" is what the organizations works towards.

TSA has more than 30 events in STEM fields and arts. These events help students learn what they want to do, and the Rudder High School supports engaging students to learn in different areas.
